Rules Changes

Alternative Crits

Moderate change

When you crit, you can choose to deal max damage + damage die or roll on the Crit table

1 No effect

2 Reduce creature's speed by 5 ft

3 +5 damage

4 Adv on next attack

5 Deafen creature for 1 min

6 +1 attack

7 Reduce creature's speed by 10 ft

8 +10 damage

9 You and 1 ally Adv on next attack

10 Blind creature

11 +1 bonus action or attack

12 Reduce creature's speed to 0

13 Crit as norma

14 Adv on next attack for entire party

15 Stun creature for 1 round

16 +1 action or bonus action

17 Crit as normal but multiply by 2

18 Paralyze creature

19 Crit as normal but multiply by 5

20 Roll for 2 effects, if one of the results is a 20 the creature is reduced to 0 HP

Alternative Food

Minor change

Food can have a bonus from +0 to +3, for each bonus your food has gives you +1d4 temp HP. adding spices to food increases its bonus by 1

Alternative Healers' Kits

Minor change

Along with the original effects of Healers' Kits, you can use a charge of a healer's kit to take a short rest in 5 minutes or regain 2 hit dice.

Alternative Improvised Weapons

Moderate change

Tiny object (Mug or bottle): 1d4 Bludgeoning, Light, Thrown(20/60)

Small object (Chair or table leg): 1d6 Bludgeoning or Piercing, Versatile(1d8)

Medium object (Chest or barrel): 1d10 Bludgeoning, Heavy, Two-handed

Sharp object (Glass shard or piece of flint): 1d4 Piercing or Slashing, Finesse, Light

Burning object (Torch or burning log): 1d4 Bludgeoning or Fire, Light, Thrown(20/60)

Thrown object (Brick or Rock): 1d4 Bludgeoning, Thrown(20/60)

Heavy thrown object (Large rock or barrel): 1d6 Bludgeoning, Heavy, Thrown(10/30), Two-handed

Liquid (Acid or Boiling Water): 1d4 Acid or Fire, Splash (5 ft radius), Thrown(10/30)

Rope or chain: 1d4 Bludgeoning or Slashing, Reach, Finesse

Large, huge or gargantuan object (Tree or horse): Damage varies by size Bludgeoning, Oversized, Reach, Two-handed

Oversized weapon property: this weapon can only be used by a creature of it's size or larger and their damage varies by the size of the weapon.

Large: 1d12

Huge: 2d8

Gargantuan: 2d10

Splash weapon property: on a hit, this weapon deals its damage to each creature in a radius designated by the feature.
