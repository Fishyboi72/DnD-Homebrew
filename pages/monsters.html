Traits

Chaotic Form: At the start of each turn, roll to change creature type and size. If there's insufficient space for the new size, adjust accordingly. Resistances granted per type:

    1: Aberration (Psychic)

    2: Beast (None)

    3: Construct (Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing)

    4: Dragon (Roll on Chaos Bolt table)

    5: Elemental (Choose two: Fire, Cold, Piercing, Slashing, Acid, Bludgeoning)

    6: Fey (Psychic)

    7: Fiend (Fire, Necrotic, Poison)

    8: Giant (Choose one: Fire, Lightning, Bludgeoning)

    9: Monstrosity (Choose two: Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing)

    10: Ooze (Acid)

    11: Plant (None)

    12: Undead (Necrotic)

Size (1d6): Tiny, Small, Medium, Large, Huge, Gargantuan

Legendary Resistance (5/Day): Succeed on a failed saving throw.

Chaotic Legendary Resistance: Roll a d20 when using Legendary Resistance:

    1: Fails, use expended

    2-19: Works as normal

    20: Succeeds without expending a use

Chaos Ready: When rolling initiative, can react to cast Chaos immediately.

Concentration Master: Can concentrate on two spells at once. Advantage on concentration saving throws.

Magic Resistance: Advantage on saves against spells and magical effects.

Magical Attacks: Attacks are considered magical.

Chaos Magic: When casting spells, roll twice on Wild Magic table; choose the better result.

Shifting Master: Defensive Shift doesn't require reaction.

Incorporeal Movement: Can move through creatures and objects. If ending turn inside an object, shunted to nearest open space.

Actions

Multiattack: The Embodiment makes four Chaos Burst attacks, or three and casts a spell.

Chaos Burst: Melee or Ranged Attack: +16 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 60 ft., one target.
Hit: 33 (4d12 + 7) damage of a type determined by current creature type:

    Aberration or Fey: Psychic

    Beast: Piercing

    Construct, Giant, Monstrosity, Plant: Bludgeoning

    Dragon or Elemental: Roll on Chaos Bolt table

    Ooze: Acid

    Undead: Necrotic

Innate Spellcasting: Spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 24, +16 to hit).
At will: Chaos, Chaos Bolt, Sorcerous Burst, Chromatic Orb

Reactions

Unerring Revenge: When the Embodiment takes more than 20 damage in one turn from a creature within 5 ft., it automatically hits that creature with a Chaos Burst attack.

Defensive Shift: When targeted by an effect that references size or creature type (e.g., Divine Smite, grapple conditions), it immediately shifts its form according to Chaotic Form. For grapples, shifts before resolution.

Legendary Actions (3/round)

Blast: Make one Chaos Burst attack.

Chaos: Cast Chaos, or if already concentrating on it, roll on Chaos's table.

Shape Shift: Shift form as described in Chaotic Form trait.

Challenge Rating: 13 (10,000 XP), Proficiency Bonus: +5

Gear: Studded Leather, Daggers (2), Sling, Pipes of the Sewers

Environment: Urban

Traits

Universal Rat Charmer: Pipes of the Sewers affect giant rats and wererats (in any form). Humanoid-form wererats must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom save or be affected.

Rat Lord: Saves to resist the pipes are at disadvantage. No half-mile limit or charge restriction. Range is 60 ft.

Supreme Rat Rallying: Swarms summoned are Large and have double HP.

Super Charming: Creatures have disadvantage on saving throws against charm effects from the warlord.

Legendary Resistance (3/day): If the warlord fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

Sewer Strikes: Weapon damage includes +6 (1d12) poison damage that ignores resistance.

Rat's Crawl: Speed is not halved while crawling.

Too Commanding: Charmed creatures act during the warlord's turn.

Rats Ready: If the warlord has any rat-like creature charmed, it has advantage on initiative rolls.

Actions

Multitask: Makes 2 dagger or sling attacks, then begins/maintains pipes of the sewers, then commands all charmed creatures to attack.

Dagger: Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack, +10 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., Hit: 15 (2d4 + 10) piercing + 6 (1d12) poison.

Sling: Ranged Weapon Attack, +10 to hit, range 30/120 ft., Hit: 15 (2d4 + 10) bludgeoning + 6 (1d12) poison.

Spellcasting: 3rd-level bard (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit).

  • Cantrips:
    • Vicious Mockery, Friends
  • 1st Level (4):
    • Charm Person, Animal Friendship, Command, Bane
  • 2nd Level (2):
    • Animal Messenger, Suggestion

    Reactions

    Swarm's Revenge: When a controlled swarm takes damage, it moves to the attacker and makes a bite attack.

    Swarm: When a creature ends its turn within 60 ft., all charmed creatures swarm it and one attacks.

    Legendary Actions (2/round)

    Befriend: Casts Friends, Charm Person, Suggestion, or Animal Friendship.

    Dagger: Makes a dagger attack; on hit, adds 6 (1d12) poison damage.

    Poison: Makes a dagger attack; on hit, the target is poisoned until end of its next turn instead of taking poison damage.

    Bonus Actions

    Rat's Nimbleness: The warlord takes two of the following actions: Disengage, Hide, Dash, Dodge.

    Lair

    The Rat Warlord resides in the sewers of a large city, such as Waterdeep. In their lair, the warlord is CR 15 (13,000 XP).

    Lair Actions (Initiative Count 20)

    Infestation: Casts Infestation on any number of targets; 4d6 damage, half on save.

    Nature's Onslaught: Casts this homebrew spell, damage is all piercing except bludgeoning on poor saves.

    Mass Command: All controlled rats/wererats make one attack. Cannot be repeated consecutively.

    Regional Effects

    Sewers within 5 miles teem with rats, giant rats, and possible wererats.

    No plant growth or non-rat animal life exists in those sewers.

    The nearby city is filthy and trash-strewn.

    Multiattack: Ganondorf makes 3 attacks, then 1 moving action.

    Eat Magic Stone: Transforms into Demon Dragon Ganondorf.

    Dodge: The next attack against Ganondorf has disadvantage.

    Legendary Actions (3 per round)

    Attack (2 actions if using bow): Makes one weapon attack.

    Move: Moves up to half speed, ignoring opportunity attacks.

    Reactions

    Retaliation (not with bow): If a creature within 30 ft. deals more than 50 damage in one turn, Ganondorf may move up to his speed and make an attack against that creature.
