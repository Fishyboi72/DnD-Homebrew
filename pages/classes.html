Subclasses

Charming Domain

Cleric subclass

Clerics that are a part of the charming domain have high charisma and generally follow the deities Aphrodite, Apollo, Loki, etc. They are very good at charming others and good at resisting it themselves. They also generally have chaotic alignments.

Charming domain spells

Cleric level       spells
1st       Charm person, Command
3rd       Crown of madness, Suggestion
5th       Enemies abound, Hypnotic Pattern
7th       Compulsion, Dominate Monster
9th       Dominate person, Geas

Bonus Cantrip

At 1st level you always know the Friends cantrip and it doesn't count against your known cantrips.


Ability Score Improvement

Also at 1st level your charisma score increases by one.


Channel Divinity: Charming Aura

Starting at 2nd level you can use your channel divinity to make you and your party look better.
As an action, you can present your holy symbol and gain a 10 ft. aura around you for 5 minutes that gives you and anyone of your choice in range +2 to charisma and when they force a creature to make a saving throw to not be charmed a -2 to the roll.


Crazy Charmer

Starting at 6th level any and all saving throws a creature makes to not be charmed by you always has -4 to the roll.


Absurd Charming

At 8th level, your charming becomes even more irresistible, whenever you force a creature to make a saving throw to not be charmed, you can force them to instead of making a wisdom saving throw to make a saving throw of any other mental stat.
You can use this ability 3 times per long rest.
You also gain immunity to being charmed.


Master Charmer

Starting at 17th level, all saving throws to not be charmed by you always have disadvantage.
Your aura also gives immunity to being charmed.

Gadgeteer

Artificer subclass

Level 3

Tool Proficiency. Gain proficiency in tinkers tools. If you already proficiency in tinker's tools gain proficiency in an artisan tool set of your choice.
Extended Spell List.

Artificer Level Spells
3rd Mage Armor, Tenser's Floating Disk
5th Cloud of Daggers, Knock
9th Phantom Steed, Sending
13th Evard's Black Tentacles, Wall of Fire
17th Hold Monster, Wall of Force

Gadget Smith.
Choose 3 of the listed gadgets to learn how to make.
You can activate a gadget as an action or bonus action if it is within 30 ft of you and can deactivate it as an action if it is within your reach. Gadgets also break down after 1 min of use. Gadget effects only take place while the gadget is activated.
During a long rest, if you have tinkers tools you may make 1 gadget or repair 2. You may only have a number of working gadgets equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum 1).
Each time you level up choose 1 gadget to learn or level up.

  • Regenerator: Each creature within 5 ft of the regenerator heals 1d4 HP at the start of their turn. +1d4 per gadget level.
  • Force Dome: a 5 ft radius see through dome surrounds the gadget. When you activate this gadget you may form an opening in the dome. If the dome is reduced to 0 HP the gadget is deactivated and destroyed. The dome is immune to all non magical damage, resistant to all damage, and has HP equal to 5x your INT (minimum 1). +5x your INT (minimum 1) HP.
  • Instant Wall: a 5 ft wide, 30 ft long, and 20 ft tall wall appears from the gadget. If a creature is occupying a space that the wall takes up, the creature takes 3d10 force damage and moves to the nearest unoccupied space. If the wall is reduced to 0 HP the gadget is deactivated and destroyed. The wall is immune to all non magical damage, resistant to all damage, and has HP equal to 5x your INT (minimum 1). +5x your INT (minimum 1) HP.
  • Auto-Turret: When you activate this gadget you may designate creatures for the turret to not target. The turret takes its turn after yours. The turret will attack the nearest creature within range on its turn. Gun: INT+PB to hit, (60/120), (1d6+INT) piercing damage. +1d6 piercing damage per level.
  • Manual Turret: When you activate this gadget you or any creature you designate may make attacks with it. Attack rolls made with this use DEX and gain a bonus equal to your INT. Gun: DEX(user's)+INT(yours)+PB(yours) to hit, (60/120), (1d6+DEX(user's)+INT(yours)).
  • Damage Emitter: Each creature within 10 ft of the gadget takes 2d6 of a damage type you choose when you activate it. +1d6 damage per level.
  • Temporal Decelerator: Each creature within up to 5 ft (you choose the radius when you activate the gadget) of the gadget is slowed granting them disadvantage on dexterity saving throws originating from outside the radius, disadvantage on attack rolls targeting creatures outside the radius, creatures outside the radius have advantage on attack rolls targeting creatures inside the radius, and each creature inside the aura has its speed halved. +5 ft max radius per level.
  • Temporal Accelerator: Each creature within up to 5 ft (you choose the radius when you activate the gadget) of the gadget is sped up granting them advantage on dexterity saving throws originating from outside the radius, advantage on attack rolls targeting creatures outside the radius, creatures outside the radius have disadvantage on attack rolls targeting creatures inside the radius, and each creature inside the aura has its speed doubled. +5 ft max radius per level.
  • Earthquaker: The ground in a 10 ft radius around this gadget becomes difficult terrain and each creature in the difficult terrain caused by this gadget must make a DEX ST at the start of its turn against your spell save DC. On a fail the creature is knocked prone.
  • Teleporter: As a bonus action you may teleport to the location of the teleporter if you are within 30 ft of the teleporter. +30 ft range per level.

Level 5

Quick Fix. Once per day you may repair a gadget you are holding as an action.
Infused Gadget. You may use one of your infusions to make a gadget. Gadgets made this way do not break down.

Level 9

More Gadgets. You may now have an additional number of gadgets made equal to your PB.

Level 15

Empowering Gadgets. Gain a bonus to your movement speed equal to twice the number of gadgets you have activated.
