Cantrips

Jack's Panic Attack

Enchantment cantrip

Casting time: 1 action
Range: 30 ft.
Components: V
Duration: 1 round

Choose a target within range. After shouting something that could cause panic, such as "did you leave the oven on?", the target makes a wisdom saving throw. On a fail the target takes 1d4 psychic damage and has no control over their movement on their turn for the duration.
On their turn, roll a d4, on any number but a 4 the target uses all their movement to move in the direction of a way to end their panic. The target wouldn't obviously run into anything harmful, like falling into a pit. The creature still can use their action and bonus action as their nature would imply.
A target who can't hear or understand you automatically succeeds the saving throw.
A target who succeeds the saving throw is immune to that phrase to cause panic for 24 hours.

When you reach levels 5, 11, and 17 the psychic damage increases by 1 die: 5(2d4), 11(3d4), and 17(4d4).

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

Pleasure Plant

Enchantment cantrip

Casting time: 1 action
Range: 30 ft.
Components: S, M (A weed leaf and a lighter)
Duration: 30 minutes

Choose a creature in range. That creature is high for the duration, and has disadvantage on any charisma or intelligence check or saving throw, but has immunity to the charmed and frightened conditions.

Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer

1st Level

Mud Bath

1st-level conjuration

Casting time: 1 action
Duration: 4 rounds
Range: 180 ft
Components: S, M (A bit of dirt and a cup of water, which the spell consumes)

Upon putting the dirt in the water (both in spells components) you summon a 20 ft radius sphere of mud within range. Any creature in the sphere's space must succeed a dex saving throw or take 1d4 bludgeoning damage or half as much on a successful one. The ground in the space of the sphere then becomes difficult terrain for the duration. Moving through the space requires 4ft for each 1ft moved instead of 2.

Using a higher-level spell slot: For every spell slot above 1st increase the damage by 1d4 and at certain slot levels the radius increases size: 4th (30ft), 7th (40ft).

Classes: Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard

4th Level

Summon Magic Weapon

4th-level conjuration

Casting time: 1 action
Range/area: 30 ft.
Components: V, M (a miniature version of the weapon conjured)
Duration: 1 minute

You choose a creature (including yourself) or spot in range and a magic weapon below of your choice appears. If you pick a creature the weapon appears in their hand(s). The weapon exists for the duration, until dismissed as a bonus action, or the weapon is farther than 1 mile from the caster.

Shortsword: 2d6
Spear: 2d6
Great club: 2d8
Greataxe: 2d10

For the purpose of resistance and immunity the weapons attacks are magical, adamantine, and silvered.
If you choose a creature and they don't have the necessary hands free, the weapon appears in the creature's space on the ground.

If you cast this spell using a 5th level spell slot or higher, for each 2 slots the weapon's damage increases by one die and the duration increases by 1 minute.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Ranger, Wizard

5th Level

Fish's Fanciful Dodge

5th-level abjuration

Casting time: 1 reaction when you are targeted with an attack or spell that isn't radiant, necrotic, poison, or psychic damage
Range: self
Components: V, S
Duration: instantaneous (1 round)

When you are targeted with an attack or spell that does a damage type other than radiant, necrotic, poison, or psychic, you do a magic infused acrobatic move reducing the damage by 5d8 + your spell casting modifier and you gain resistance to that attack type until the start of your next turn.
If an attack does bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage to trigger this it only gives you resistance to nonmagical attacks of that type.

If you cast this spell using a 6th level spell slot or higher the amount reduced increases by one die for each slot above 5th.

Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard

Nature's Onslaught

5th-level enchantment

Casting time: 1 action
Range: 90 ft.
Components: S, M (10+ rations, which the spell consumes)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You call upon animals to come and attack your foes.
Animals come and fill a 30ft sphere within range. Any creature of your choice within the circle makes a dex saving throw, taking 2d6 piercing, 2d6 slashing, and 2d6 bludgeoning damage on a fail. On a success, it instead takes half damage.
If a creature fails by 5 or more it is knocked prone and takes an additional 2d6 bludgeoning damage.
Whenever a creature starts their turn within the sphere, they must succeed another dex saving throw or take 2d6 piercing, slashing, or bludgeoning damage (your choice when you cast the spell).
If there aren't enough animals within a mile (at the DM's discretion) the slot is expended, the spell fails, but the material components are not consumed.

For every slot above 5th, one of the initial damages increases by 1d6 (your choice).

Classes: Druid, Ranger

6th Level

Nature's Ward

6th level abjuration

Casting time: 1 action
Range: touch
Components: V, M (An axolotl tail in a gilded walnut costing 10 gp+)
Duration: 1 hour

Choose a school of magic; a willing target that you touch becomes immune to any spell of that school for the duration.
If it is included in an area of effect, it appears to just succeed saving throws from it and remain unharmed.
If it is targeted with an attack roll or a spell that targets creatures directly, the spell seems to work but the creature remains unaffected.
If the target is targeted by a jumping spell, such as chain lightning, roll to hit it as normal, but the target just takes no damage on a hit.

Classes: Druid

7th Level

Drain Life

7th-level necromancy

Casting time: 3 rounds
Range/Area: 60 ft
Components: V,S
Duration: Concentration, 1 minute

You point your finger at a creature within range. If you manage to hold your concentration for the duration you drain the target's life force until they have only 1/2 of their previous hit points.
If you ever can't see the target the spell ends.

Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
